The Icon of Taipei: Taipei 101

Taipei 101 is a magnificent landmark that reaches into the clouds. Its unique structure resembles a giant stalk of bamboo, symbolizing growth and strength. Visitors often gather at the observation deck to enjoy the amazing view of the city. Inside, a massive gold wind damper protects the tower from a disaster like an earthquake. The building’s design reflects a blend of modern technology and traditional culture. Every New Year’s Eve, a spectacular fireworks display lights up the night sky. It remains a proud symbol for people in Taiwan.

中文對照翻譯

台北101是一座伸向雲端的宏偉地標。它獨特的結構像一株巨大的竹子，象徵著成長與力量。遊客經常聚集在觀景台，欣賞這座城市令人驚嘆的美景。在建築內部，一個巨大的金色風阻尼器保護著大樓免受地震等災難的影響。這座建築的設計體現了現代科技與傳統文化的融合。每年的跨年夜，壯觀的煙火表演都會點亮夜空。它仍然是台灣人民引以為傲的象徵。
