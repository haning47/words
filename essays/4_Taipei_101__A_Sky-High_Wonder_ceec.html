Taipei 101: A Sky-High Wonder

Taipei 101 is a fantastic landmark that towers over the city. It is recognized as the most famous tourist attraction in Taiwan. Engineers showed great determination when building this skyscraper to resist strong earthquakes. Its massive height allows the structure to dominate the entire urban skyline. Every aspect of the design combines modern engineering with authentic cultural symbols. Visitors often ask staff to clarify how the massive wind damper keeps the tower stable. After an eventual elevator ride to the top, the panoramic view is truly breathtaking.

中文對照翻譯

台北101是座傲視全城的極好地標。它被公認為台灣最著名的旅遊景點。工程師在建造這座摩天大樓以抵抗強震時，展現了極大的決心。它巨大的高度使這座建築俯瞰整個城市天際線。設計的每個方面都將現代工程與正宗的文化符號相結合。遊客經常請工作人員闡明巨大的風阻尼器是如何保持大樓穩定的。在最終搭乘電梯到達頂樓後，全景景觀確實令人嘆為觀止。
