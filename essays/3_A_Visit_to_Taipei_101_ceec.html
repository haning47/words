A Visit to Taipei 101

Visiting Taipei 101 is truly enjoyable for everyone. This attractive building stands tall in the middle of the city. Even on a humid afternoon, many tourists gather to admire its unique design. The tower has a considerable height that reaches high into the clouds. Visitors love to explore the large shopping mall located inside. It continues to attract people from all over the world every year. The staff there always show a polite attitude toward every guest. It is a landmark that represents the beauty of Taiwan.

中文對照翻譯

參觀台北101對每個人來說確實是令人愉快的。這座有吸引力的建築在城市中心屹立不搖。即使在一個潮濕的下午，許多遊客也會聚集在一起欣賞其獨特的設計。這座塔有相當大的高度，直達雲端。遊客喜歡探索內部的型購物中心。它每年繼續吸引著來自世界各地的人們。那裡的員工對每位客人都表現出禮貌的態度。它是代表台灣之美的一個地標。
